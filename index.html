terrain and fishing in South Carolina

 

Fishing in South carolina is very unique. There are many different types of terrain in South Carolina

Theres flat land, mountain land, and hilly land. Also you can be right by the ocean one minute and

then drive two hours your in more hilly land as you get farther away, but drive another two hours

and your in complete mountains. It is for sure unique to its own. So what all of these different

terrains mean is different types of fishing for each terrain. For example on the coast of south

carolina you have salt water fishing, anywhere from Red Drum to Marlin. And then near more

of the midlands you have fishing in more swamp type areas for fish such as Largemouth Bass

and Catfish. And lastly you have fishing in the upstate where a lot of people go to catch Trout.

There are many types of Trout in South Carolina you can catch such as the Rainbow, Brook,

and Brown Trout. Dont get me wrong there are many other types of fishing in South Carolina, but

these seem to be the most popular
